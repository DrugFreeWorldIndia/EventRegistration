FAQ's

Q: How to get registration ID?

A: After registering, participants will get all the relevant information such as registration ID,and so on.

Q: Is there a registration fee for the competition?

A: Yes, the registration fee for each participant is INR 400.

Q: How will the winners be selected?

A: Winners will be selected by a panel of judges based on creativity, technique, and adherence to the theme.

Q: How to submit my painting?

A: All entires to be submitted on or before 20 July,2023. The Entries must arrive at the Drug-Free World, India's New Delhi Office by Speed Post/courier within the given time frame and in the specified format.

Q: Can anyone participate in the painting competition?

A: No, the competition is only open to people between the ages of 13 to 18.
